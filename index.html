What is a demo?

A demo is a shortened form of the word 'demonstration'. It is a presentation of a product, service or idea that is intended to showcase its features, functionality and benefits.

Why do companies use demos?

Companies use demos to showcase their products or services to potential customers, investors, or partners. It is a great way to highlight the unique selling points of the product and help in making informed purchase decisions.
